Recipe #1

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Ground beef 2 ibs
Cheese 2 slides
Tomatoes 2
Onions 1
Buns 4

Step 1: Smash the beef patty on the grill and when it looks good enough flip it over and slap on it with a slide of cheese.

Step 2: Put sliced onion rings on top with lettuce and tomatoes.

Step 3: Toast the buns then put them together.

Recipe #2

Ingredients Quantity
Jajang Noodles 2 packs
Black bean paste 1 can
Onions 2
Pork (diced) 2 ibs
Carrots 3 ibs

Step 1:Stir fry the vegetables till they are all cooked. Put the black soybean paste in with sesame oil.

Step 2:When the sauce is thicken, boil the noodles for 10 minutes.

Step 3:When the noodles is done boiling, put them in a bowl and pour the sauce over. Finished with some sliced cucumbers on top.

Recipe #3

Ingredients Quantity
Shrimp 3 ibs
Bread crumbs 3 ibs
Egg 1
All purpose flour 2 ibs

Step 1:Pour the powder and bread crumbs on two seperate plates or tray.

Step 2:Beat the egg then soak the shrimps from the order powder - egg - bread crumbs.

Step 3:When we are done, set the temperature to medium otherwise they will burn before they are even cooked. Then gently put the coated in poweder into the frying pan. You should be done within 15 minutes.
